# MAD9020 Deliverables

Every week there will be work for you to complete inside or outside of class. Each assignment will be a block in a design system that will allow you to create your midterm and other projects.

Week 1 - User needs & business goals

Following Tuesday by midnight

In Week 1, we delve into understanding and defining the user needs and business goals for a full website vs mobile app.

Following Tuesday by midnight

Following Tuesday by midnight
